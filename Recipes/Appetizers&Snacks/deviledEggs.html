Deviled Eggs

Home

Ingredients

  • 6 - Eggs
  • 3 T - Mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp - Dijon Mustard
  • 1 tsp - Apple Cider Vinegar
  • Salt, To Taste
  • Black Pepper, To Taste
  • Paprika, garnish

Steps

  1. Prepare the eggs
    1. Place eggs in a pot of water and then bring it to a hard boil
    2. Let boil for 3 minutes
    3. Remove eggs from the water and put in bowl of ice water
    4. Peel the eggs
  2. Cut all the eggs in half and separate the whites from the yolks
  3. Add the egg yolks to a separte bowl along with the mayonnaise, mustard, vinegar salt and pepper
  4. Stir the yolk mixture until well combined
  5. Spoon the yolk mixture back into the egg whites and garnish with paprika and black pepper
