Egg Drop

Ingredients

  • 0.5 gal - Water
  • 3 - Chicken thighs or drumsticks
  • 1 - Medium yellow onion
  • 1 cup - Mixed vegtables (peas, corn, and carrots)
  • 1 T- Black pepper
  • 2 T- Rice flour (or corn startch)
  • 5 - Eggs
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil and add chicken, let cook for about 20min
  2. Scrape the foam off the top of the bowl and add the whole onion, chopped
  3. Once the onions turn clear remove the chicken
  4. Add the mixed vegtables and black pepper and leave to boil for another 20min
  5. Mix rice flour with 1 egg and pour into the soup and stir to thicken
  6. Beat the rest of the eggs
  7. Bring the soup back to a boil and pour the beat eggs into the soup in a circular motion, creating long strings of eggs
  8. Turn off the heat and let stand for 20 min and then serve
