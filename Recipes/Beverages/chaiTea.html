Chai Tea

Home

Ingredients

  • 3 cups - Water
  • 2 - Cinnamon Sticks
  • 1 1/2 tsp - Cardamom Seeds
  • 10 - Cloves
  • 2 - Star Anises
  • 1 tsp - Ginger, Ground
  • 1 1/2 tsp - Nutmeg, Ground
  • 6 - Black Tea Bags
  • 1 tsp - Vanilla Extract
  • 6 T - Honey

Steps

  1. Add water cinnamon sticks, cardamom seeds, cloves, star anises, ginger, and nutmeg to a pot and bring to a boil
  2. Reduce heat and let simmer for 5 minutes
  3. Add the tea bags and steep for 5 minutes
  4. Remove the tea bags and strain the spices
  5. Pour back in pot and add vanilla and honey
  6. Stir until dissolved and chill in the fridge

Iced Latte:

  1. Fill cup with ice
  2. Add 1 cup Chai concentrate and 1 cup milk

Notes

  • Can use 6 cups of water instead to make more, less spicy concentrate
  • Can Add 2 Tbl of sugar and 1 Tbl of brown sugar on top of the honey, but dont add all 6 Tbl of honey
