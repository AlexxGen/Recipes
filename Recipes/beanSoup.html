Bean Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 lb - White Bulgarian Beans
  • 3 - Poblano peppers
  • 1 - Tomato
  • 1 Tbs - Chubletsa (Blugarian Spice)
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the Beans
    1. Place the beans in a pot of water and bring to a boil
    2. Allow to boil for 10 minutes, then strain the water
    3. Repeat step 1 and 2 to fully clean and cook the beans
  2. Prepare the Peppers
    1. Over open flame if possible, char the pepper skin and cook the peppers until limp
    2. Peel the skin off the peppers
  3. Bring the beans to boil in a pot of water again
  4. Add spices
  5. Add the baked peppers and the tomato (chopped thickly) and boil for 10 minutes
