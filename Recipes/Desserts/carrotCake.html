Carrot Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups - All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 tsp - Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp - Baking Soda
  • 1 1/2 tsp - Cinnamon, Ground
  • 1/2 tsp - Ginger, Ground
  • 1/4 tsp - Nutmeg, Ground
  • 1/2 tsp - Salt
  • 3/4 cup - Oil
  • 4 - Large Eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups - brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup - Granulated Sugar
  • 1/2 cup - unsweetened applesauce
  • 1 tsp - Pure Vanilla Extract
  • 3 cups - grated carrots

Cream Cheese Frosting

  • 1 (8-ounce) Pkg - Brick Style Cream Cheese, Softend
  • 1/2 cup - Unsalted Butter, Softened
  • 2 cups - Powdered Sugar
  • 1 tsp - Pure Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350° F
  2. Brush two 9" round baking pan with butter and flour or use parchment paper
  3. In a large bowl, combine oil, eggs, brown sugar, granulated sugar, applesauce, and vanilla extract
  4. Add flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and salt
  5. Stir in the carrots
  6. Split batter between the two prepared baking pans
  7. Bake each for 30 to 35 minutes, and test with a toothpick
  8. Remove the cakes from the oven and allow to cool for about 20 minutes before removing them from the pan
  9. Transfer cakes to a wire rack to finish cooling before frosting
  10. Place one cake on a plate and fully frost. Then layer the second cake and finish frosting

Cream Cheese Frosting

  1. Using a mixer, beat cream cheese until smooth
  2. Add butter and mix until well combined
  3. Add powdered sugar and vanilla extract and continue to mix until fully combined
