Baklava

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs - Walnuts, chopped
  • 1/2 cup - Granulated Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp - Cinnamon
  • 1/8 tsp - Cloves, ground
  • 28 sheets - Filo dough
  • 1 cup - Butter, softened

Syrup

  • 2 cups - Granulated Sugar
  • 1 cup - honey
  • 2 cups - Water
  • 1 - lemon peel

Steps

  1. Combine walnuts, sugar, cinnamon, and cloves.
  2. Brush 12" x 17" baking pan with butter.
  3. Place 6 buttered filo sheets on the bottom.
  4. Sprinkle with 1/3 of the walnut mixture.
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 until the walnut mixture runs out (should be 2 more times)
  6. Top with 10 filo sheets and brush with remaining butter.
  7. With a very sharp knife, score filo into 1 1/2" diamonds or squares.
  8. Bake in a preheated 350°F oven for 45 minutes to 1 hour, or until golden brown.
  9. Cool slightly and pour warm syrup evenly over baklava
  10. Makes 88 pieces.

Syrup

  1. Bring all ingredients to a boil, then simmer for 10 minutes.
  2. Stain and cool slightly, then pour warm syrup over baklava.
  3. Allow it to cool completely. Cut and serve.
