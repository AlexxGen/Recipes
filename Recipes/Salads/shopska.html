Shopska Salad

Home

Ingredients

  • 1/2 - Medium Red Onion, Sliced
  • Salt, To Taste
  • 3 - Tomatoes, Chopped
  • 1 - Cucumber, Chopped
  • 3 T - Oil
  • 1/2 cup - Bulgarian Feta

Steps

  1. Add the onions to a bowl with salt (about 1/2 tsp) and smash by hand until they release their juices
  2. Stir in the tomatoes, cucumber, and oil
  3. Top with feta and serve

Notes

  • Variations include adding 1 - chopped baked pepper, 2 T - freshly chopped parsely, and/or 1 - chopped avacado
