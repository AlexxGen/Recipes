Olive Garden Salad (Mock)

Home

Ingredients

  • 1/2 head - Iceburg Lettuce, Shredded
  • 2 - Tomatoes, Sliced
  • 10 - Olives, Sliced
  • 1/4 - Red Onion, Sliced
  • 5 - Pepperoncini
  • 3 T - Parmesan
  • Olive Garden Dressing, To Taste

Steps

  1. Combine lettuce, tomatoes, olives, red onion and pepperoncini in a bowl and toss
  2. Top with parmesan and dressing
