Green Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch - Green Onions
  • Salt, To taste
  • 1 head - Roman Lettuce
  • 1 tsp - Apple cider vinager
  • 2 Tbs - oil

Steps

  1. Slice the green onions and add them to a bowl
  2. Sprinkle with salt, about 1/2 tsp
  3. Smash the onions by hand until they release their juices
  4. Slice the lettuce and add it to the bowl of the smashed onions
  5. Smash the lettuce the until they also release their juices
  6. Drizzle with the oil and vinager and serve
