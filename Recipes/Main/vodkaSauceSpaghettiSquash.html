Vodka Sauce Spaghetti Squash

Ingredients

  • 1 - Spaghetti Squash
  • Salt, To Taste
  • Black Pepper, To Taste
  • 3 T - Oil
  • 1 - Medium Yellow Onion, Chopped
  • 3 - Garlic Cloves
  • 3 1/2 cups - Tomato Paste
  • 1/3 cup - Vodka
  • 1/4 cup - Basil, Fresh Chopped
  • 3/4 cup - Half and Half
  • Parmesan, To Taste

Steps

  1. Cook the Spaghetti Squash
    1. Cut in half and scoop out the seeds
    2. Drizzle oil on the cut side and sprinkle with salt and pepper
    3. Place the halves face down on a baking sheet and bake for 40 minutes on 350° F
    4. Remove from oven and allow it to cool slghtly
  2. Prepare the Spaghetti sauce
    1. In a sauce pan over medium heat, saute the the onion and garlic in the oil
    2. Cook until soft
    3. Add tomatoe paste, vodka, salt, and black pepper and reduce heat to let simmer for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally
    4. Add basil and stir in half and half
    5. Continue to cook until warmed through
  3. With a fork, scrape the inside of the spaghetti squash halves to get the veggie noodles
  4. Dish out the veggie noodles, add the desired amount of sauce, top with parmesan, and serve

Notes

  • Serve with Green Salad
