Procupine Meat Loaf

Ingredients

  • 1 lb - Pork, ground
  • 1 lb - Beef, ground
  • 5 - hard boiled eggs, peeled
  • 1 Tbs - Chubletsa (Blugarian Spice)
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mix the meat and spices in a bowl.
  2. form the meat around the egg in a tear drop shap and put on a baking sheet.
  3. Add pepercorns as seen fit for the eyes and nose.
  4. Add raw noodles to the back for the quills.
  5. Repeat steps 2-4 for all other porcupines.
  6. Put in the oven, and bake for 00 min at 000° degrees.
