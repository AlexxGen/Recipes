Procupine Meat Loaf

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 lb - Pork, Ground
  • 1 lb - Beef, Ground
  • 5 - Eggs
  • 1 Tbs - Chubletsa (Blugarian Spice)
  • Salt, To Taste
  • Black Pepper, To Taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the eggs
    1. Place eggs in a pot of water and then bring it to a hard boil
    2. Let boil for 3 minutes
    3. Remove eggs from the water and put in bowl of ice water
    4. Peel the eggs
  2. Mix the meat and spices in a bowl
  3. form the meat around the egg in a tear drop shap and put on a baking sheet
  4. Add pepercorns as seen fit for the eyes and nose
  5. Add raw noodles to the back for the quills
  6. Repeat steps 2-4 for all other porcupines
  7. Put in the oven, and bake for 20 min at 350° F
