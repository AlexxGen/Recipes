Pull Apart Cinnamon Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 cup - warm Water (110°F)
  • 1 tsp - Yeast
  • 1/4 cup - granulated sugar
  • 3 cups - Flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp - salt
  • 3 T - Milk
  • 1 - egg
  • 5 T - Butter, softened

Sauce

  • 1/3 cup - Butter
  • 1/2 cup - Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 cup - vanilla Ice Cream

Steps

  1. Make the Dough:
  2. Add the warm water, yeast, and a Tbs of sugar to a pot and let sit until bubbly (about 15 minutes).
  3. Add flour, remaining sugar, salt, milk, egg, and butter, and stir until dough is formed.
  4. Let rise until dough doubles in size (about 1 hour).
  5. Roll out dough into a rectangle.
  6. Spread butter, and sprinkle with brown sugar and cinnamon.
  7. Slice rectangle into 32 little rectagles.
  8. Greese two 9" x 2" bread pan and pour some sauce to cover the bottom.
  9. Make two stacks, 16 each, of the little rectangles and slide into the bread pans.
  10. Drizzle the remaining sauce on top of the loaves.
  11. Let rise until loaves double in size (about 1 hour).
  12. Preheat the oven to 350°F and bake for 20 minutes.
  13. Let cool and serve.
  14. Makes 2 loaves.

Sauce

  1. Melt the butter and brown sugar in a sauce pan.
  2. Once the butter sugar mixture is creamy stir in the ice cream.
  3. Once ice cream melts pour some sauce to cover the bottom of the buttered pan.
