Pull Apart Cinnamon Bread

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup - olive oil
  • 2 - yellow onions, finely chopped
  • 4 - cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 large russet potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 6 cups - chicken or vegtable broth
  • 3/4 lb - kielbasa or cooked sausage, sliced
  • 1 bunch - kale
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper
  • Extra-virgin olive oil for drzzling

Steps

  1. In a large, heavy pot, warm the olive oil over medium heat
  2. Add the onions and garlic and saute until golden (about 5-7 minutes)
  3. Add the broth, cover, and bring to a boil
  4. Reduce the heat to a simmer and cook until the potatoes are tender (about 20 minutes)
  5. Remove from the heat and let cool slightly
  6. Working in batches, coarsely puree the soup in a blender leavinf some potato slices intact
  7. Retrn to the pot
  8. Add the sausage, cover, and simmer over medium heat until the sausage is heated through (about 5 minutes)
  9. Add the kale and cook, uncovered, until wilted but still bright green (about 3-5 minutes)
  10. Season with salt and peper and serve, drizzeled with extra-virgin olive oil
  11. Serve with fresh cornbread
