Soupatopshita

Home

Ingredients

  • 1/2 G - Water
  • 1 whole - Onion, diced
  • 1/2 lb - 70% 30% ground pork and beef mix
  • 1/2 cup - rice
  • 1/2 cup - flour
  • 2 - potatoes, diced
  • To Taste - tomato, diced
  • To Taste - pepper, diced
  • To Taste - Salt
  • 1 tsp - Chubletsa (Bulgarian spice)
  • 1 pinch - Peppercorns
  • 1 cup - plain yogurt
  • 1 - egg
  • To Taste - fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Combine water, onion, and a tsp of salt in a soup pot and boil
  2. In a separate boil, combine ground meat mix and rice
  3. Roll meat into 1/2 inch meat balls and coat in flour
  4. Boil soup until yellow
  5. While in a rolling boil add the balls to the soup one at a time
  6. Add the potatoes, tomatoes, pepper, chubletsa, peppercorns and let boil until the potates are almost done, about 20 min
  7. Remove from heat and allow to cool slightly
  8. In a separate bowl, mix yogurt and egg until creamy
  9. Add a ladle of soup into the mixture and slowing combine the mixture into the pot of soup while stirring, Don't break up the the egg like in egg drop soup
  10. Top with parsley and serve

Notes

  • The mix can also be 100% ground pork
