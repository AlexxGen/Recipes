Pelachinki

Ingredients

  • 2 cups - Flour
  • 1 tsp - Salt
  • 1/3 cup - Oil
  • 1/4 cup - Milk
  • 2 - Medium Eggs
  • 1 cup - Water

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, combine flour, salt, oil, milk, and eggs and stir until smooth
  2. Add in the water and any extra needed to make a runnier consistency
  3. Heat up a skillet to medium heat, drizzle some oil in the pan, and pour out a thin layer of batter
  4. When the pelachinki starts to come away from the edges, flip it and continue cooking until golden brown on both sides
  5. Remove from pan and serve warm
  6. Makes about 16 pelachinki

Notes

  • Can also serve pelachinki rolled with honey
