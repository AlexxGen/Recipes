Pancake

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup - Plain Yogurt
  • 1 tsp - Baking Soda
  • 2 cups - Flour
  • 1/2 cup - Sugar
  • 1 tsp - Salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp - Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp - Vanilla Extract
  • 1/4 cup - Oil
  • 1/2 cup - Milk
  • 2 - Medium Eggs
  • 1/2 cup - Water

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine yogurt and baking soda and set aside
  2. In a medium bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt, baking powder
  3. Add the vanilla extract, oil, and eggs and stir to form a smooth thick batter
  4. Add in the water and any extra needed to make a slightly runnier consistency
  5. Fold in the the yogurt mix from step one (it should have bubbled and doubled in size)
  6. Heat up a griddle or skillet to medium heat and pour batter out in medium sized circles
  7. When they start to bubble flip and continue cooking until done.
  8. Serve with syrup and butter, or a fruit jam
  9. Makes about 14 to 16 pancakes.

Notes

  • For additional fluff, whip the egg whites separately and fold into the batter after adding the yogurt mix
