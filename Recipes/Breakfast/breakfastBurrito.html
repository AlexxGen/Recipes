Breakfast Burrito

Home

Ingredients

  • 3 - Medium Potatoes, Diced (1/2 inch peices)
  • 2 T - Oil
  • 3 - Eggs
  • 2 - Flour Tortillas
  • 6 T - Mild Cheddar Cheese, Grated (or whatever cheese of choice)
  • 4 T - Picante Salsa

Steps

  1. Fry the potatoes in the oild til golden brown
  2. Add the eggs and continue cooking until the eggs or done
  3. Split the grated cheese between the two tortillas
  4. Split the the potato-egg mixture between the two totillas and top with the salsa.
  5. Roll and serve
