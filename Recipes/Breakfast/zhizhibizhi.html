Ingredients

  • 5 slices - Sourdough Bread
  • 5 - Eggs
  • Salt, To Taste
  • Black Pepper, To Taste
  • Paprika, To Taste
  • 1/2 cup - Oil

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs in a separate bowl and stir in the salt, black pepper, and paprika
  2. Heat the oil in a deep skillet on medium heat
  3. Dip a slice of bread into the egg mixture until both sides are fully coated
  4. Add the eggy bread to the hot oil and cook both sides until golden brown
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 for all slices of bread
